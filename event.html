Eaton Changing Gears

The event that is carrying forward the legacy of Abhivridhhi, Eaton, a month-long session by industry and faculty experts that covers a wide range of soft skills and brain-boosting exercises to ensure a smooth transition and bridges the gap from campus to corporate world.

Mock GDPI

The "Mock Group Discussion and Personal Interviews" event is designed to provide students with valuable skills and confidence necessary for excelling in group discussions and personal interviews. These skills are highly significant for success in the professional world.

LinkedIn Building & Mentorship Program

An intensive workshop that trains students to gain self-awareness, networking skills, and knowledge about leveraging LinkedIn and its use as a professional tool. Attendees become aware of the platform's immense potential and learn the art of showcasing their achievements.

Resume Building

Participants have the opportunity to attend a comprehensive workshop focused on resume building. During this session, they get to learn about the essential elements of an effective resume and how to tailor it to suit specific job roles or industries. Expert trainers share their valuable insights to help participants create resumes that stand out.
